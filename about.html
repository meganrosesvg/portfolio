HI, I'M MEGAN ROSE

A 32 year old teacher-turned-designer who
is passionate about helping others succeed.

As a former teacher, I have had years of experience helping users solve problems, teaching the same lesson at different angles for different learners, and being patient, kind, and unbiased in my approaches.
I am also a lifelong creative. My whole life has been full of pens, paper, paint, pastels, cloth, clay, wood, and glass. In 2004, I taught myself how to use Photoshop. Creating and editing digitally was new to me and I couldn’t get enough.
In 2015, I gave birth to my clever little monster, Jacob. Every day, I strive to make his life experience the best it can be. I have to be on my toes, and I have to remember to put my child’s perspective first as I help him learn.

I began to miss being able to reach, help, and listen to others on a daily basis. As the first step in my solution, I researched about my interests, abilities, and passions. Soon I began teaching myself Illustrator and began helping small businesses with branding.
Then in 2018, I started a UX/UI apprenticeship. I created an incredibly user friendly cloud storage service for photographs called Photosphere. Soon I hope to be designing on a daily basis outside the home, and I know my abilities will continue to amaze.
